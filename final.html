CSC 106 Air Hockey Final Project

For our final project, we chose to code air hockey. We have a one player option, and then instructions on how to play. You use the mousepad to control your paddle with your player inside of the paddle. You have three different levels that you can choose from. If you increase the level, the CPU gets faster and faster making it harder to win. If you get 7 goals, you win the game, and if the CPU gets 7 goals, you lose the game.
